⚠️ Common Scam Examples

Learn to identify fake payment links

🚨

Brand Spoofing

Fake brand prefixes pretending to be real payment apps

  • gpay-reward-free.com
  • paytm-bonus-cashback.com
  • phonepe-verify-account.com
🔤

Misspellings

Common typos used to fool users

  • recieve (receive)
  • casback (cashback)
  • reword (reward)
📎

Suspicious Extensions

Unusual domain endings commonly used by scammers

  • .xyz, .click, .top
  • .download, .stream
  • .tk, .ga (free domains)
🔗

URL Shorteners

Hidden URLs that mask the true destination

  • bit.ly/claim-reward
  • tinyurl.com/verify
  • ow.ly/bonus

🛡️ Stay Safe - Best Practices

Essential tips to protect yourself from scams

1

Never Share PIN

Your UPI PIN should NEVER be entered on any website. Only use official apps.

2

Don't Click Unknown Links

Even if a link looks official, verify by calling the organization directly.

3

Use Official Apps

Always download payment apps from official app stores only.

4

Verify Sender

Banks never ask for OTP or PIN via SMS/Email. Be skeptical of urgent requests.

5

Check URLs Carefully

Scammers use similar-looking domains. Always check the complete URL.

6

Enable Two-Factor

Add extra security by enabling 2FA on all your online accounts.
