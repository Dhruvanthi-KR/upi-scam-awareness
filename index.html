UPI Fake Link Scam Awareness

Learn how scammers trick people using fake payment links

What is a UPI Scam?

UPI scams happen when fraudsters send fake payment links through WhatsApp, SMS, or email. These links look like real payment requests but are designed to steal your money.

Example of Fake UPI Links

  • https://upi-reward-cashback.xyz
  • https://receive-money-upi.click
  • https://gpay-reward-free.com

⚠ Official UPI apps never send links like this.

Example of Scam Message

📩 Congratulations! You received ₹5000 cashback. Click the link below to claim your reward.

https://upi-reward-cashback.xyz

⚠ This is a fake message used by scammers.

Check if a Link is Suspicious

How to Stay Safe

  • Never enter UPI PIN on websites
  • Do not click unknown links
  • Use only official apps
  • Verify sender before making payment
